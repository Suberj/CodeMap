About CodeMap

CodeMap is a coursework-focused programming support website designed to help college students move from classroom theory to practical coding confidence.

Our Mission

We want to give students one centralized place to learn the programming languages they need for academic success. By organizing installation help, tutorials, and documentation guidance into clear learning paths, CodeMap reduces the time students spend searching for scattered resources and increases the time they spend actually learning.
